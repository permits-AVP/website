Our Solar Permitting & Design Solutions

Comprehensive residential and commercial permitting services.

Site Plan & Roof Layout Design

Precise roof layouts based on installer and homeowner inputs. Matches US standards and local AHJ requirements.

  • Roof measurements & Array layout
  • Fire setback spacing
  • Obstruction marking & Racking placement
  • Roof plan (CAD + PDF)

Electrical Single Line Diagram (SLD)

Clear and NEC-compliant electrical design for permitting. Compliant with NEC 2017 / 2020.

  • Inverter configuration & DC stringing
  • Breaker/Disconnect sizing
  • Rapid shutdown & Grounding detail
  • AC wiring diagram

Permit Package Preparation

Complete permit-ready drawing sets for AHJs and utilities. Includes Load calculations.

  • Cover sheet, Site plan, Roof plan
  • SLD & wiring diagrams
  • Mounting & racking details
  • Structural & Electrical Stamps Available

Bill of Materials (BoM)

Accurate material lists for purchasing & installation.

  • Module count
  • Rails & mounting hardware
  • Disconnects & breakers
  • Conductor & conduit sizing

As-Built Drawings

Final drawings after installation for record keeping & utility submission.

  • Final array layout
  • Actual electrical routing
  • Updated SLD
  • Revised equipment list

Revision & Update Support

Fast revisions to keep your project moving. Turnaround: 6–12 hours.

  • Inverter/model changes
  • Layout adjustments
  • Wire sizing corrections
  • AHJ-requested markups
